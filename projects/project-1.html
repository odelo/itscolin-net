Overview

I've long wished to create a low powered hardware solution for opening thumbturn door locks. Almost everyone has lived in a place with them or still lives in a place with them. It came to mind in my first off-campus apartment with my now fiance after we had gotten our cat. We found ourselves constantly looking for a cat sitter when we'd go out of town and always had to coordinate key handoff and pick up. An automatic solution seemed nice and I was inspired as a fledgling computer engineering student. Alas, it never went anywhere. Not much time for tinkering.

Fast forward 5 years, I'm actually a computer engineer with much more knowledge (and money). Lets take a crack at it.
